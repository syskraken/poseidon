KRAKEN PRIME — WHAT THE BOT DOES WHILE FARMING

When you press START BOT, the bot repeats this raid loop forever until stopped:

1. Dismiss popups on the home screen (okay buttons, and it even opens the free
   Blacksmith chest if one appears).
2. Tap the Attack button, then "Find a Match", then confirm the attack.
3. Scout the base: it reads the available GOLD using OCR. If the gold is below
   your Minimum Gold Target it taps Next and checks the following base — up to
   20 skips, then it attacks whatever comes next.
4. Lightning phase: it scans the base for air defenses and drops your
   configured lightning spells on them (e.g. 4 per air defense).
5. Deploy troops at your pinned deploy points, spreading taps across all
   points, then deploys heroes (one tap each).
6. Rage phase: waits your configured Rage Delay, then drops rage spells one by
   one on your pinned rage points.
7. Waits for the battle to end (taps the fast-forward button when it appears,
   then Return Home / claim reward, and clears all post-battle popups). A
   battle is given up to 10 minutes before it is considered stuck.
8. Waits a few seconds for troops to retrain, then starts the next raid.

The Dashboard counts every raid ("TOTAL RAIDS") and shows uptime and live logs.
Raid History lists each raid with its start time.

Self-recovery: if the Attack button cannot be found (game frozen, disconnected,
"village is under attack" screens, etc.), the bot force-closes Clash of Clans
through ADB, relaunches it, waits 30 seconds for it to load, clears popups and
continues farming on its own. Screenshot failures automatically trigger an ADB
reconnect and retry.

Important expectations:
- The emulator window must stay open. Don't play in it while the bot runs.
- The bot taps blind coordinates for deployment, so if you change your army
  composition (different number of troop types, added/removed heroes or
  spells), re-pin your troop bar slots or the wrong slots will be selected.
- Attack strategy is simple mass-deploy farming (e.g. mass dragons). It is
  built for loot farming, not for 3-star pushing.
